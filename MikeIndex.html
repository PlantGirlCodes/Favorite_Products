About the Grill

The Weber Q 2200 grill is a high-quality and versatile propane gas grill that is designed to deliver exceptional grilling experiences for outdoor enthusiasts. Known for its compact size and portability, this grill is perfect for small spaces, camping trips, picnics, and tailgating events. Despite its size, the Weber Q 2200 boasts a powerful 12,000 BTU burner that ensures even heat distribution and efficient cooking.

Equipped with a large cooking area, it allows grilling for a small group or family gathering with ease. The porcelain-enameled cast-iron cooking grates offer superior heat retention and produce beautiful sear marks on the food. The built-in thermometer on the lid enables precise temperature monitoring, ensuring consistent results every time.

One of the standout features of the Weber Q 2200 is its electronic ignition system, making it quick and hassle-free to start up the grill. The side tables provide convenient space for food preparation and essential grilling tools.

The grill's durable construction, featuring a cast aluminum body and a glass-reinforced nylon frame, ensures long-lasting performance and resistance to weather elements. Cleaning up after a delightful grilling session is effortless, thanks to the removable catch pan.

Overall, the Weber Q 2200 is an excellent choice for grill enthusiasts who seek a compact, portable, and high-quality grill that delivers outstanding results and makes outdoor cooking an absolute delight.
